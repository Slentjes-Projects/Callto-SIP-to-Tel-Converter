Privacy Policy

Summary: This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Callto/SIP to Tel Converter Browser Extension

This privacy policy describes how the "Callto/SIP to Tel Converter" browser extension handles user data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data.

This extension:

  • Does NOT collect any personal information
  • Does NOT track your browsing history
  • Does NOT store any data locally or remotely
  • Does NOT communicate with any external servers
  • Does NOT use cookies or any tracking mechanisms
  • Does NOT access any data beyond what is necessary to convert links on the current page

How the Extension Works

The extension operates entirely within your browser. It:

  1. Scans the current webpage for links that start with "callto:" or "sip:"
  2. Converts those links to "tel:" format for better phone compatibility
  3. This process happens locally in your browser and no data leaves your device

Permissions

The extension requires permission to run on all websites solely to detect and convert callto: and sip: links. No other permissions are requested or used.

Third Parties

This extension does not share any data with third parties because it does not collect any data.

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
